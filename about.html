About Us

Who We Are

Handmade by Em is a small, woman-owned business focused on creating handmade accessories to allow people of all types and backgrounds to find an accessory they enjoy.



Our Mission

Handmade by Em seeks to not only provide accessories that will speak to all types of individuals, but we also seek to provide for the community that we are so lucky to be a part of. We hope to be able to give back to the community through providing employment to women and other minority groups, as well as an outlet for aspiring designers to be able to share their designs through physicial products.



Women-Founded

Handmade by Em was founded by Emily, a young woman in her early twenties who works full-time in a pharmacy, while going to school for a second degree. With the little free time she had, Emily started creating hair scrunchies, cosmetic bags, and tote bags, by watching Youtube tutorial videos on how to sew.
