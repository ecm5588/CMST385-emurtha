Discography


Debut Album: 2 Cool 4 Skool


The BTS debut album contains the group's first singles, "No More Dream" and "We Are Bulletproof Pt. 2" which are both hardcore 90's hip-hop tracks about prejudice against the group for not being like everyone else and the inner feelings of the members as the group is introduced to the world.
Release Date: June 2013
