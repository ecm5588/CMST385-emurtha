Frequently Asked Questions

On this page, you will find the answers to the most anticipated Frequently Asked Questions regarding the launch to Handmade by Em. If you cannot find your answer here, please feel free to email us at the bottom of the page!

FAQ Answer
When will Handmade by Em launch? Handmade by Em will be launching following the completion of my second undergraduate degree in late 2024, early 2025, when I am able to dedicate a large portion of time to growing and expanding the business.
What kinds of products will Handmade by Em sell? Handmade by Em will sell all kinds of cute, trendy, handmade accessories to fit everyone. These will include:
  • scrunchies

  • pillows

  • cosmetic bags

  • tote bags

  • other accessories
Who is the target audience of products made by Handmade by Em? Products by Handmade by Em will be made for a wide variety of people, in hopes that everyone will be able to find something they like on the site. The subcategories that will exist on the site for each type of accessory will include, but will not be limited to:
  1. Girls

  2. Boys

  3. Women

  4. Men

  5. Pets
